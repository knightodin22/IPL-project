Prashant Solanki

About


Prashant Solanki, a talented Indian cricketer, has emerged as a reliable batsman for Chennai Super Kings. Born on September 5, 1990, in India, Solanki made his IPL debut in 2023 and has quickly made his mark in the league. As a specialist batsman, Solanki brings stability to the CSK batting lineup with his consistent performances. With 16 IPL matches under his belt, he has showcased his ability to build innings and accelerate when needed. Beyond IPL, Solanki aspires to contribute significantly to Indian cricket and aims to represent the country at the highest level.
