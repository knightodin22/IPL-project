Mukesh Choudhari

About


Mukesh Choudhary (born 6 July 1996) is an Indian cricketer who plays for Maharashtra in domestic cricket and for Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League.[1] He made his first-class debut for Maharashtra in the 2017–18 Ranji Trophy on 9 November 2017.[2] He made his List A debut on 7 October 2019, for Maharashtra in the 2019–20 Vijay Hazare Trophy.[3] He made his Twenty20 debut on 8 November 2019, for Maharashtra in the 2019–20 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy.[4] In February 2022, he was bought by the Chennai Super Kings in the auction for the 2022 Indian Premier League tournament.[5].
