Ravindra Jadeja

About


Ravindra Jadeja, often referred to as "Sir Jadeja" by fans, is an Indian international cricketer known for his exceptional all-round skills. Born on December 6, 1988, in Navagam-Khed, Gujarat, Jadeja is primarily a left-arm orthodox spin bowler and a left-handed batsman. He made his IPL debut in 2008 and has since then been a crucial player for Chennai Super Kings. With a total of 184 IPL matches under his belt, Jadeja has showcased his prowess in both batting and bowling, earning him a reputation as one of the finest all-rounders in the cricketing world. In addition to his IPL stints, Jadeja represents Saurashtra in domestic cricket and has been a vital asset to the Indian national team across all formats. His agile fielding and accurate throws have also made him a standout performer in the field. Off the field, Jadeja is known for his calm demeanor and philanthropic efforts, contributing to various social causes.
